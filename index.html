Our Donate Plan

This fund will be used to provide food and shelter for the victims mainly. In some serious case we can provide medicine and treatment.

Food For Everyone

Most of the money (around 60%) from fund will be used to distribute food. We suggest to buy dry food or instant food since there is no chance to cook during flood. Food will be delivered by our volunteers at a specific place. In the case of over flooded area we hire a helicopter for distributing food.

Shelter For Everyone

Our rescue tean has already on their mission to rescue people. We will use government building such as- local school building. Many mosque authorities have allowed us to use mosque as a shelter of homeless people. Rescue team already rescued 800 people. They are staying in serveral school buildings.

Medicine and Treatment

Because of poluted water most of the victims are suffering from diarrohea, dysentry, fever etc. Our volunteers are trying their best to provide proper medicine and treatment. But we are running out of medicine and equipement. It is impossible to refund medicine without your help
